About me

Hi all, my name is David Masclet, i am a software engineer and i really like GIS and all the stuff around it. (see my CV in french). I also like agility (SCRUM,XP).

I have started Gisgraphy on November 2007, the initial goal of the project was to offer a free, open source and easy way to use Geonames data.

Step by step, i have build the framework, first an importer with an IHM, then a geolocalisation and a fulltext webservice, a back office with statistics, ...

Then i thought that an opensource geocoder can be useful, so i extends the framework with an importer for Openstreetmap and develop a webservice too.

My goal was to :

  • provide and support a lot of languages (XML, json, PHP, ruby, python, Atom, RSS / GeoRSS , more to come)
  • A high quality software with high test coverage
  • Easy to install, (a minimum to configure)
  • Fully internationalized, with a lot of languages supported
  • Provide an Opensearch module


I hope it is the case. I am currently the only developer.
